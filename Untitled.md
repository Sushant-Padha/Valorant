We have,
$$
\begin{align}
a + b &= 39 \tag{1} \\

ab & = 324 \tag{2} \\ \\
\end{align}
$$
We know that,
$$
\begin{align}
(a - b)^2 &= a^2 + b^2 - 2ab \\
&= (a^2 + b^2 + 2ab) - 4ab \\
&= (a + b)^2 - 4ab \\
&= (39)^2 - 4(324) \tag{From (1) and (2)} \\
&= 225
\end{align}
$$
$$
\therefore~~~ a - b = \pm15
$$
